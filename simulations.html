All Circuit Simulations

Below is the complete collection of interactive circuit simulations I've created. Each simulation runs in your browser and allows you to adjust the speed from micro-second increments to real time.

Resistors in Series
Explore how resistors behave when connected in series. Observe voltage division and current flow.
Resistors in Parallel
Investigate parallel resistor configurations and current division principles.
DC RC Transient Circuit
Observe capacitor charging and discharging in an RC circuit. Watch the exponential voltage changes.
DC LC Transient Circuit
Watch the oscillation behavior in an inductor-capacitor circuit. See energy transfer between L and C components.
Zener Regulated Linear PSU
Explore how a Zener diode creates a stable voltage reference in a power supply unit.
Cascaded Op-Amp "Guitar Pedal"
This amplifier design puts the op-amp into saturation and features diodes to unevenly clip the positive and negative sides of the sinusoid, creating distortion effects.
BJT Voltage-Divider Bias
Study the voltage-divider bias configuration for a Bipolar Junction Transistor, essential for amplifier design.
J-K Flip-Flop
Explore the behavior of a J-K flip-flop, a fundamental sequential logic circuit element.
D Flip-Flop
Investigate the operation of a D flip-flop, commonly used for data storage in digital systems.
